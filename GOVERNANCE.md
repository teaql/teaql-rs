# TeaQL Project Governance

This document outlines the governance model, roles, and responsibilities for the TeaQL project.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Maintainers
Maintainers are responsible for reviewing pull requests, merging code, managing releases, and guiding the project's technical direction. Maintainers have write access to the repository.

### Contributors
Anyone who submits issues, pull requests, or helps with documentation is a contributor. We welcome and value all contributions.

## Bus Factor and Access Continuity
To ensure the long-term sustainability of the project, we maintain a "bus factor" of at least 2. This means that multiple independent individuals hold administrative privileges and release credentials. No single point of failure exists regarding project access.

## Decision Making
Decisions are primarily made by consensus among maintainers. For significant architectural changes, an RFC (Request for Comments) process may be used.

## Code of Conduct
All participants are expected to adhere to our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
